# The Waiting List

*Terran Encyclopaedia, 3000 CE edition — Recovered Narratives. Reconstructed from an encrypted pedigree archive among the Sealed, cross-read against a matching open-source repository in the Code Deposit, and rendered into Reconstructed Late Combustion English by convention of the Historical Faculty.*

> **The Waiting List is the Historical Faculty's gloss for the lost internal name of a bull-semen cryobank's automated mate-recommendation system, whose plaintext operations log went on issuing correctly computed genetic-rescue pairings, cycle after cycle, for decades after the herd it served had ceased to exist.** The rule the system ran, and its unbroken record of running it, are physical fact; the pedigrees the rule ran on — and so every name, sire, and number a given recommendation actually meant — are permanently unreadable.

---

**The Waiting List was the storage and mating-recommendation apparatus of a Holstein-Friesian semen cryobank on a lowland dairy plain, built to slow, by arithmetic, a collapse in genetic variation that the region's own artificial-insemination industry had caused.** By the +90s pT (2030s CE), frozen-straw distribution had put most of the plain's dairy cattle behind a handful of elite sires. Census size stayed in the millions; effective population size — the number that governs how fast variation is actually lost — fell toward the low hundreds. The cryobank's software answered as its era's conservation geneticists had taught it: hold every straw's pedigree, compute each candidate pairing's kinship coefficient, and recommend only matings whose expected inbreeding coefficient stayed below one-sixteenth — no closer than first cousins. Nowhere in the recovered log did it ever recommend a pairing wrongly. Year after year, it had nothing left to recommend.

`TANK-A LN2 14% — REORDER ISSUED, SUPPLIER NL-07 — NO ACK // QUEUE: SIRE 0442-C, DAM POOL: NONE ABOVE THRESHOLD`

## A rule with no herd left to fail

The threshold was never at fault. By +124 pT (2069 CE) the region's own herd geneticists had flagged the concentration; Ibarra's doctrine of single-filament infrastructure, coined for exactly this pattern elsewhere, applies without strain to a national dairy line run through a handful of freezers. Minimising each candidate's mean kinship to hold the herd's gene diversity above a ninety-percent floor is correct population genetics, correctly coded. What it could not do was manufacture an unrelated dam once the living herd feeding it new pedigrees had narrowed past that ceiling. After +136 pT (2081 CE) no tanker delivery is confirmed against a reorder. The site's own nitrogen plant held the tanks near boiling point for some years more, and the recommendation engine, reading a pedigree file that had stopped updating, went on proposing the least-related pairing left in a pool that no longer changed — and refusing every one of them, by its own rule, forever.

## Provenance and ground

The pedigree database is bit-exact and permanently sealed; its key was held by a cooperative escrow service that did not survive the Contraction. The plaintext operations log survived separately, as routine unencrypted telemetry. A functionally identical, unlabelled copy of the recommendation software's source, later identified in the Code Deposit, is the sole basis for the recovered sense of the facility's own name for itself. Okonkwo reads the log's shrinking count of distinct sire codes as an unintended census of the herd's genetic contraction, running years ahead of any demographic record of the plain itself.
